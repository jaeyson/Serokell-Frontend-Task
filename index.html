Heritage

Italian food varies greatly depending on what part of the country you're in. Get to know more about the northern parts of Italy — from the jagged mountains of the Dolomites to the beautiful coastline of Liguria — and try recreating the dishes that make them culinary hotspots.

Our growing community of like-minded foodies get to share the kitchen with our Chefs, while learning how the Family prepares our best-sellers.
